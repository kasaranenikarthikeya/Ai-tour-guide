About AI Tour Guide

Discover the USA like never before with AI Tour Guide—a smart companion that harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to guide you through iconic destinations across all 50 states.

Built with Flask and powered by AI, this project is more than just a travel tool—it’s a passion project born from a love for exploration and technology. Whether you’re dreaming of hiking majestic mountains, strolling through historic sites, or relaxing on sun-kissed beaches, AI Tour Guide brings personalized insights and seamless navigation to your fingertips. Features like state-by-state searches, a favorites list, dark mode, and multilingual support make every journey a breeze.

We’re Kalyan, Jaswanth, and Karthikeya—a trio of developers united by a shared vision. As avid travelers and tech enthusiasts, we set out to create something that blends cutting-edge AI with the joy of discovery. From brainstorming late-night ideas to coding the perfect user experience, this app reflects our dedication to making travel smarter, easier, and more inspiring.

Join us on this adventure—explore the United States, uncover hidden gems, and let AI Tour Guide be your guide. Have feedback or just want to say hi? We’d love to hear from you!
